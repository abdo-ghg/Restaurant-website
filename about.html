About Our Restaurant

The story behind Korba Square's finest dining experience

Our Story

Welcome to مطعم غازي و شركائه, where passion for food meets professional excellence. Founded by a group of ambitious CS students, our restaurant represents the perfect blend of technical precision and culinary artistry.

What began as a side project alongside our professional careers has grown into one of Korba Square's most distinguished dining establishments. We created this restaurant because "الحوجة أم الاختراع" (necessity is the mother of invention), and our need to express creativity beyond code led us to the culinary world.

Today, we're proud to serve a diverse menu that celebrates local flavors while incorporating modern techniques and presentations. Our restaurant has become a gathering place for those who appreciate thoughtful, innovative cuisine in an elegant yet comfortable setting.

Our Philosophy

The principles that guide our culinary journey and define our exceptional dining experience

Quality Ingredients

We source the freshest, highest quality ingredients from local producers, ensuring that every dish represents the best of what our region has to offer.

Authentic Recipes

Our menu pays homage to traditional culinary techniques while incorporating contemporary innovations. We honor authentic recipes that have been perfected over generations.

Hygiene Standards

Ensuring highest standards of Hygiene is a must for us. Using highest quality equimpent and tools, our restaurant is recognized as one of the best restaurants in Cairo.

Welcoming Atmosphere

We've created a space where everyone feels welcome and comfortable. From the decor to the service, every aspect of your experience is designed to make you feel at home.
